Smoked Sausage Mac & Cheese

Description

I found the original version of this recipe online, which used shredded chicken. However, I did not have any chicken breasts at the time and decided to instead substitute with some andouille chicken sausage instead. I use Kraft box mac and cheese for this recipe as it saves me time, but you could make your own mac and cheese and use it just as easily if you choose.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Kraft mac & cheese
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 sliced smoked sausage link
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon parsley flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In an oven safe pot prepare Kraft mac and cheese according to instructions on the box with an extra 1/4 cup of milk
  3. While boiling water for mac and cheese, cook sliced sausage in a skillet over medium heat until browned (ensure the sausage has reached an internal temperature of 165)
  4. Once the Kraft is prepared, set 1/2 cup of shredded cheese aside and combine the remaining ingredients in the oven safe pot
  5. Flatten out the top of the mac and cheese in the dish and cover the top with the remaining cheese
  6. Bake for 15 minutes at 350
  7. Enjoy

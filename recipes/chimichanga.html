Baked Chicken Chimichangas

Description

I only really ever make two dishes and they are both the other two recipes that I have listed here on this site. Therefore I ran out of ideas for my third recipe to list here. For that I asked my friend if he had any recommendations for recipes this led me to these chimichangas. While I cannot personally vouch for this dish I am a chimichanga fan and these sound amazing.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1 pound shredded chicken
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • Some sort of cooking oil spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix cream cheese, pepper jack cheese, and taco seasoning
  3. Add shredded chicken to the mixture
  4. Divide the mixture to the 8 tortillas
  5. Roll tortillas into burritos and place on a baking sheet with the seams on the bottom
  6. Spray the tops of the chimichangas with the cooking oil
  7. Bake chimichangas for 30 minutes, flipping after 15 minutes

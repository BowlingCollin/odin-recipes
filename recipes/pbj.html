The Best Spicy Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

I discovered this version of the traditional peanut butter and jelly Sandwich just a short while ago. My version includes an unusual fourth ingredient which is sriracha hot sauce. I had thought of this recipe shortly after reading a reddit post about an individual that discovered that the reason he enjoyed the spicyness of a peanut butter and jelly sandwich due to his allergy to peanuts. After discovering this allergy he began to use jalapeno jelly and peanut butter substitute, to imitate the feeling created from the allergy. This concept was interesting to me, therefore leading me to begin eating a spicy peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Wheat bread
  • Crunchy peanut butter
  • Raspberry preserves
  • Sriracha hot sauce

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter one slice of bread
  2. Spread your preserves on the other slice of bread
  3. Put sriracha onto the peanut butter
  4. Put the two slices of bread together
  5. Enjoy
